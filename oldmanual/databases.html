Chapter 2: Databases

Pretty much all Xapian operations revolve around a Xapian database. Before performing a search, details of the documents being searched need to be put into the database - the search process then refers to the database to determine the best matches for a given query. The process of putting documents into the database is usually referred to as indexing.
